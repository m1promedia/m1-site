Short-form Video Editor
Montreal, QC Full-time Part-time Freelance Contract

Are you a highly creative, bilingual (French/English) editor with short-form video experience? We need a strategic editor who can expertly pivot between distinct brand aesthetics — from fashion Reels to professional finance Shorts.

The Core Role: Strategic Social Editing
  • Client Content Production: Edit raw footage into polished, high-retention vertical videos, ensuring the tone and style perfectly match each client's brand.
  • 🇫🇷/🇬🇧 Bilingual Assets: Meticulously apply dynamic text, captions, and trending visuals (jump-cuts, SFX) in both English and French content.
Mandatory Requirements (Non-Negotiable)
  • Proven Social Video Experience: A strong portfolio that clearly showcases your ability to produce high-retention, vertical short-form video content across varied styles and tones.
  • Professional Bilingual Fluency: Essential for editing and applying text/captions in both French and English (spoken and written).
  • Strategic Insight: Deep functional knowledge of social media video dynamics, algorithm performance, and current trends (and when to use them).
  • Editing Expertise: Skill in an industry-standard NLE (e.g., Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, CapCut).
  • Agile Work Ethic: Ability to manage multiple concurrent projects and meet tight deadlines.
Advantageous Skills
  • Familiarity with basic motion graphics (After Effects) or graphic design tools (Canva/Photoshop).
  • Experience with compliance and regulatory considerations (e.g., finance industry content).

Background: 0–2 years of professional editing experience. Substantial freelance or robust personal projects focused on client-style briefs are highly valued. If you're ready to translate diverse client needs into powerful, professional video performance, apply and share your portfolio today.

  • Expérience Avérée en Vidéo Sociale : Un portfolio solide qui démontre clairement votre capacité à produire du contenu vidéo vertical court format à fort taux de rétention, dans des styles et des tons variés.
